The Birthday Adventure

It's your birthday, and you're feeling adventurous! You decide to go on a hike in the nearby woods, where you hear there's a secret waterfall that's only accessible by a hidden trail.

You pack a backpack with some essentials and set out on your journey. As you approach the entrance to the woods, you see two paths: one is well-trodden and wide, while the other is narrow and overgrown. Which path do you take?
