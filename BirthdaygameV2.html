The Birthday Adventure - Harry Potter Edition

It's your birthday, and you've received an invitation to attend a secret event at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. You've always dreamed of visiting Hogwarts, so you accept the invitation and travel to the castle.

When you arrive, you're greeted by a group of Hogwarts students who explain that the event is a scavenger hunt, and you're the guest of honor. You'll need to solve riddles and complete challenges to find the Philosopher's Stone, a powerful magical artifact that has been hidden somewhere in the castle.

Excited to start your adventure, you follow the students into Hogwarts and begin your search for the Philosopher's Stone...
